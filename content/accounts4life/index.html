To validate a business idea, Terry needed a prototype that allowed users to route money between many sources and destinations completely intuitively, be rock-solid with an obvious and slick user experience, and parse existing bank transaction files (used by all banks today to transfer money). All on a start-up budget.

The prototype was a resounding success and successfully proved the business as not just viable, but an obvious investment.
